COTTAGE PRAYER MEETINGS

Give thanks to the Lord, for he is good! His faithful love endures forever : Psalm 118:29

Our members get together to praise and worship and to learn more about the faith on the last Saturday of every month. Prayer Schedule, venue and address for 2015 will be emailed to the members. Please contact our Prayer Coordinator Mathew Varghese for any questions, suggestions or arranging a prayer meeting at your residence.
