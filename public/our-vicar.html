OUR VICAR

Rev. Fr. Dr. Daniel Thomas

 

Background

Rev. Fr. Dr. Daniel Thomas is the son of the late Mr. M. M. Daniel and Annamma Daniel of Munjanattu, Kozhencherry, Kerala, India.

Education

  • B.A, (Philosophy and Psychology) University of Kerala, India
  • B.D (Bachelor of Divinity) Leonard Theological College and Serampore College, Serampore, India
  • M.A (Religion) Andover Newton Theological School and Centre for World Religions at Harvard Divinity, USA
  • M.A (Indian Philosophy and Religions) McMaster University, Hamilton, Ontario, Canada
  • Ph.D .( Indian Philosophy and Religions) McMaster University, Hamilton, Ontario, Canada

Fr. Dr. Thomas served as the executive officer responsible for Research and publication at Christian Institute for the Study of Religion and Society,Bangalore India. He also served as Assistant professor of Philosophy and religious Studies at Mount Allison University, Sackville, New Brunswick, Canada. In July of 1972, he was appointed as Professor and the Head of the Department of Philosophy and Religious Studies at Cégep Vanier College, Montréal, Québec, Canada. He also served on the advisory board of the education Department of the Quebec Government representing the College.

Publications

Several scholarly essays of the author have been published in different international magazines and journals. Books published are listed below.

      • Sree Narayana Guru, Published by C.I.S.R.S, Bangalore, 1964
      • The Concept of Soul, C.I.S.R.S, Bangalore, 1971
      • The Fundamentals of Indian Culture, Sophia Publications, 1989
      • Towards A Peaceful Society, Vanier College Press, Montreal, 1990
      • Meaningful and Fruitful Life, Chistava Sahitya Samiti (CSS), Thiruvalla. 2007
      • Meditation: A Technique to keep You Happy and Healthy, CSS, Thiruvalla, 2010

Ordination

He was ordained as Apodyaqno by His Grace Philippose Mar Eusebius on July 22nd, 1995. On Thursday July 27th, 1995, H.G. Philippose Mar Eusebius ordained him as Shamosho. His Holiness Moran Mar Baseliose Marthoma Mathews II, the Catholicose, ordained him as Kassiso at St. Mathews’ Orthodox Church, Kozhencherry, Kerala, India on July 29th, 1995.

Family

Married to Sosamma , the daughter of Mr.Ittoop and Annamma of Chakkanakuzhile, Kolencherry. They have two children, Priya & Pran and a grandson, Roshan.
